### Deep-Dive: Why OIS and Super AMOLED Matter

Most mid-range phones in this price bracket omit **Optical Image Stabilization (OIS)**. In the Galaxy A24, OIS physically moves the camera sensor to compensate for hand shakes. For Kenyans who film content for TikTok or capture family moments while walking, this results in significantly clearer photos and smoother videos compared to digital-only stabilization.

The **Super AMOLED screen** is another standout. Unlike standard LCDs found in many competitors, AMOLED displays offer "true blacks" and a 1000-nit peak brightness. This means even under the bright midday sun in Nairobi or Mombasa, your screen remains perfectly readable and vibrant.
