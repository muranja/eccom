## HP EliteBook 820 G3 Price in Kenya - Compact & Affordable

The **HP EliteBook 820 G3** is the smaller sibling of the popular 840 G3. With a 12.5-inch screen, it fits easily into any handbag or small backpack, making it the top choice for students and professionals constantly on the move.

### Why Buy the 820 G3?
**Ultra-Portability:** Weighing significantly less than the 14-inch version, you won't even feel it in your bag.
**Same Power, Smaller Size:** It packs the exact same Core i5 processor and 8GB RAM as the larger 840 G3, so you lose no performance.
**Insane Value:** For around 16k, it's one of the cheapest ways to get a proper SSD-equipped Windows laptop.
